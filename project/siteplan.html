Site Name:

Crohn's and Ostomy Alliance

This name represents what I am looking to acheive which is a landing place for people who have Crohn's Disease and and Ostomy and are looking for a supportive and educational community.

Optional domain availability: CrohnsandOstomyAlliance.com

Site Purpose

This site is meant to provide hope to those recently diagnosed with Crohn's Disease, or those who have longterm Crohn's Disease and are new to the Ostomy Life. It also is meant to provide resources for them to get ostomy bags, as well as tips and tricks and links to helpful videos. This site is also meant to create a community of people to help encourage one another in this new struggle and ultimately give them hope for the future.

Scenarios

  • What are some resources that I need for life as a new ostomate?
  • What are some tips and tricks to make life easier with this disease or with an ostomy?
  • Is there somewhere I could go to get samples of supplies?
